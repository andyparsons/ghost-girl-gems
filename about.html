Care & Guarantees

Everything you need to know about your gems — how long they last, what's covered, and how to keep them sparkling.

Replacements

If a tooth gem falls off within 2 weeks of application, the client may receive a replacement free of charge for up to a week after notifying the shop/artist of fallen gem(s).

Guarantees

Tooth gems are guaranteed to last 2 weeks at a minimum but usually stay on for at least a month. Depending on size, placement, and a client's individual habits, gems can last anywhere between 1 and 6 months (sometimes longer!).

Tooth gems are applied with a process that is completely dental safe, using all dental grade products and adhesives.

Aftercare & Removal

During the first 24 hours after receiving tooth gems, be gentle when brushing teeth and avoid eating hard foods and candy. These are foods that may apply pressure to areas with gems, like apples, chewy candy, and crusty breads. Additionally, during the first 12 hours try to avoid acidic foods and beverages.

To make gems last longer, continue to brush teeth more gently around areas with gems and avoid using electric toothbrushes.

Tooth gems should fall off on their own. Since they are made of Swarovski, they are non-toxic and safe to swallow.

To remove tooth gems sooner, you may go to a dentist or pick them off at home. Both options will not cause any damage to the tooth or enamel.
